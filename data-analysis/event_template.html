Correlation

Line chart: Compares the trend of stock prices and sentiments for the given period of time. For the stock price the daily value considered is the closing price of the day. Prices are normalized by setting the maximum to 1 and scaling down the others. For sentiment the degree of positivity for each day is calculated as follows: 1 * (positive tweets)% + 0.5 * (neutral tweets)% + 0 * (negative tweets)%. The obtained values are normalized. Hovering on each datapoint reveals the actual rates for both datasets.

Correlation coefficient: Shows the statistical relationship between the stock prices and positivity, regardless of the day of the event.
